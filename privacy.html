Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

WiseCal ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

a) Calendar Data

  • We access your device calendar through Apple EventKit to create and manage appointments
  • Calendar data stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers

b) Screenshots and Images

  • When you share a screenshot, it is temporarily processed to extract text
  • Images are sent to Google Gemini AI for text analysis
  • Images are not stored after processing

c) Usage Analytics

  • We collect anonymous usage data through Firebase Analytics
  • This includes: app opens, feature usage, and error reports
  • No personally identifiable information is collected

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To extract appointment information from screenshots
  • To create calendar events on your behalf
  • To improve app performance and user experience

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For text extraction and date parsing
  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics
  • Apple EventKit: For calendar integration

5. Data Retention

  • Screenshots: Deleted immediately after processing
  • Analytics data: Retained for 90 days
  • Calendar events: Stored locally on your device

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Disable analytics in device settings
  • Request data deletion by contacting us

7. Children's Privacy

WiseCal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page.
